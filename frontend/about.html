Your Mental Wellness Companion

Mirra AI is a safe, empathetic space where you can express your feelings without judgment. Always here — ready to listen, remember, and gently support you through whatever you're going through.

✍️ Why We Built This

"Most of us can't afford therapy. So we end up talking to ChatGPT at 2am — because it's the only thing that's always there."

That thought started this whole thing. We're two engineering students who've been there — scrolling through AI chatbots at odd hours, not because we wanted to talk about code or productivity, but because we just needed someone to listen.


The thing is, those general-purpose AIs help — but they're not built for this. They don't remember that last week you were anxious about the same thing. They don't gently check in. They don't know when to suggest breathing exercises versus when to just listen.


So we built Mirra. Not as a replacement for therapy — but as the 3am friend who always picks up. One that actually understands mental health, remembers your journey, and meets you where you are. Because everyone deserves support, not just people who can afford it. 💜

💜 Our Mission

Mental health support should be accessible to everyone — any time, any day, without a price tag or a waitlist. Mirra AI was built with one purpose: to be a compassionate, non-judgmental presence in your life. Whether you're feeling anxious, overwhelmed, sad, or just need someone to talk to — we're here with open arms and a listening heart.
